About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led institute focused on digital, cyber and AI risk. It bridges academic research with real industry projects to train professionals for modern technology risk environments. The Institute of Digital Risk connects research, education and industry collaboration to build future cyber and AI risk professionals.
